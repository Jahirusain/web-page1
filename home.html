NARUTO

Naruto is a Japanese manga series and anime television series about a young ninja who wants to become the leader of his village:

Manga:

Written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, the manga was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine from 1999 to 2014.
The story is told in two parts, with the first part covering Naruto's pre-teen years and the second covering his teens.
The manga sold over 250 million copies worldwide.

Anime:

The anime series aired in the early 21st century.

Story:

Naruto Uzumaki is a ninja-in-training from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.
He's treated with disdain by the adults in the village due to a secret surrounding his birth.

Other related items:

There are also video games and a trading card game based on Naruto.

ONE PIECE

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda that follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates. The story is about the pirates' search for the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates.

Here are some highlights from the story:

The treasure

The One Piece is the name given to the treasure that was once owned by the Pirate King Gol D.Roger.
The treasure is said to be of unimaginable value and is located on Laugh Tale, the final island of the Grand Line.

The crew

The Straw Hat Pirates are the crew that Monkey D. Luffy leads in his search for the One Piece.

The flashback

In one early chapter, Luffy's backstory is shown in a flashback.

The Noland flashback story

Some say that the Noland flashback story is a great example of Oda's storytelling skills, with great buildup and twists.

One Piece has been serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 1997, and as of July 2024, the individual chapters have been compiled into 109 tankōbon volumes.
It has also been adapted into a live-action series on Netflix.
