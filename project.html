Introduction

Naruto is a Japanese manga series and anime television series about a young ninja who wants to become the leader of his village:

Manga:

Written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, the manga was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine from 1999 to 2014.
The story is told in two parts, with the first part covering Naruto's pre-teen years and the second covering his teens.
The manga sold over 250 million copies worldwide.

Anime:

The anime series aired in the early 21st century.

Story:

Naruto Uzumaki is a ninja-in-training from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.
He's treated with disdain by the adults in the village due to a secret surrounding his birth.

Other related items:

There are also video games and a trading card game based on Naruto.
